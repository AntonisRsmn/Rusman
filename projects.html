Zepp Discord Bot

Zepp is a small Discord bot created using JavaScript. My goal with it was to become verified on discord and help users moderate their servers while having fun with all the extra features I added, such as games and music, so you can listen while you chat or play with your friends. You can try Zepp through the website at https://zepp.gr.
